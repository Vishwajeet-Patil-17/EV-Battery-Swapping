What is Battery Swapping?

Battery swapping is a method where a depleted electric vehicle battery is replaced with a fully charged one. This reduces waiting time and increases EV efficiency.

Why Battery Swapping?

  • Time Efficiency: Swapping takes less than 5 minutes.
  • Reduced Infrastructure Load: Less demand on charging stations.
  • Battery-as-a-Service: Users don’t need to purchase expensive batteries.
  • Fleet Optimization: Perfect for taxis, delivery vehicles and buses.

How It Works

Battery swapping typically takes under 5 minutes. Automated systems detect and safely remove the empty battery.

Drivers enter the station, swap the depleted battery with a charged one, and continue driving.

Types of Battery Swapping

Manual Swapping

Technicians manually replace the battery.

Automated Swapping

Robotic systems replace batteries in minutes.

Mobile Swapping Vans

Portable battery swap services for fleets.
